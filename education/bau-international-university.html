BAU International University

  • Founded: 2015
  • Batumi

Overview

Siberia is the home of OMSK State Medical University. It was founded in 1920 and became the prime source of higher medical education in the region. It is among the most trending and oldest universities in Siberia. More than 1,000 faculties have been awarded “Excellence in Health” and “Honored Doctors of Russian Federation”. It is the perfect choice for studying MBBS in Russia. Today the OMSK University is among the largest medical institution in the Siberian Region and Russia as a whole. The medical program of the university is certified and recognized by the Ministry of Education Russian Federation. It is also one of the most demanding NMC-approved Medical universities in Russia for studying the MBBS program. OMSK has highly experienced medical faculty for delivering the best program to applicants. They have the latest equipment to provide practical training to students. This university is the leading medical and preventive institution in OMSK city and the region. It is one of the best universities in and a perfect choice for studying MBBS in Abroad .
