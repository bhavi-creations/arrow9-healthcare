About the University of Perpetual Help System DALTA

The University of Perpetual Help System DALTA is a private, non-sectarian university that was established in 1975 in Las Piñas, Metro Manila, Philippines. The university offers a wide range of undergraduate and graduate programs across various disciplines, including medicine, nursing, dentistry, law, engineering, and business, among others. The UPHSD has several campuses across the Philippines, including:

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Q1. What is the duration of the MBBS program at University of Perpetual Help System Dalta?

The MBBS program at UPHSD Philippines is for a duration of five and half years, including one year of clinical internship.

Q2. What is the medium of instruction at University of Perpetual Help System Dalta?

The medium of instruction at UPHSD is English, which makes it easier for Indian students to study and communicate with their peers and faculty members.

Q3. Is the campus of University of Perpetual Help System Dalta safe and secure?

Yes, the campus of UPHSD is safe and secure. The university has security personnel and CCTV cameras installed to ensure the safety and security of students.

Q4. What are the eligibility criteria for admission to the MBBS program at University of Perpetual Help System Dalta?

To be eligible for admission to the MBBS program at UPHSD, students must have completed their 10+2 education with a minimum of 50% marks in Physics, Chemistry, and Biology.
