Kazakh National Medical University

  • Founded: 1963
  • Kazakhstan

Overview

Kazakh National Medical University is a leading medical institution located in the city of Almaty, Kazakhstan. It was founded in 1963 and is one of the oldest and most prestigious medical universities in Kazakhstan. Kazakh National Medical University is recognized by the Medical Council of India (MCI) and the World Health Organization (WHO). The university offers a range of undergraduate and postgraduate medical programs including medicine, dentistry, nursing, and pharmacy. One of the most popular programs at Kazakh National Medical University is the Bachelor of Medicine, Bachelor of Surgery (MBBS) program, which is taught entirely in English. This program is designed to provide students with a comprehensive understanding of medical science and clinical practice. The MBBS in Kazakhstan at Kazakh National Medical University is particularly attractive to international students who wish to study medicine abroad, as it is affordable, internationally recognized, and offers excellent career prospects. Kazakh National Medical University has state-of-the-art facilities including modern laboratories, research centers, and a large library with a vast collection of medical books, journals, and research papers. The university has highly qualified and experienced faculty members who are dedicated to providing the best possible education and training to their students. Students who enroll in the MBBS in Kazakhstan at Kazakh National Medical University undergo a rigorous and comprehensive curriculum that includes both theoretical and practical components. The program is designed to equip students with the knowledge, skills, and practical experience required to become successful medical practitioners.

Kazakh National Medical University Ranking

Kazakh National Medical University has consistently been ranked as one of the top medical universities in Kazakhstan and the Central Asian region. In the latest QS World University Rankings by Subject (2022), Kazakh National Medical University was ranked #451-500 in the world for Medicine.
The university is also recognized by various international organizations such as the World Health Organization (WHO), the Medical Council of India (MCI), and the Educational Commission for Foreign Medical Graduates (ECFMG), which is a testament to the quality of education provided by the university. Kazakh National Medical University has been accredited by the Independent Kazakh Agency for Quality Assurance in Education (IQAA). It has been recognized as a leading university in Kazakhstan by the Ministry of Education and Science of the Republic of Kazakhstan.
