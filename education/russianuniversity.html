Russian Universities medical program with their application system

There are so many big countries namely Russia, the UK, the USA, Canada, etc around the world provide so many big courses, few of them are BBA, MBA, MBBS, etc. Talking about the MBBS course i.e Bachelor of Medicine and Bachelor of Surgery course is one of the highly rated courses around the world. Doing MBBS in Russia is the perfect place just not for students who are of the same origin but also for those who want to study an MBBS abroad. So many abroad students want to study MBBS in Russia or want to study MBBS abroad as they are not satisfied with their country’s Education System.

For complete guidance, there are so many highly ranked colleges provided in the country which give the required direction to the individual’s career. Some of them are

  • Voronezh State Medical University
  • Crimea Federal University
  • Northern State Medical University
  • Tver State Medical University

About Voronezh State Medical University

It is a public research university located in the city of Voronezh, Russia. It is one of the country’s oldest educational institutions. The university was founded in 1930. Voronezh State Medical University is a member of the Russian Association of the classical instutions and is recognized by the Ministry of Education and Science of the Russian Federation. The university is approved or recognized by WHO (World Health Organization) and NMC (National Medical Commission).

So if someone looking to study MBBS in Russia Voronezh State Medical University is a perfect choice. University is also the best place for those students who want to study MBBS abroad as they are not satisfied with their country's education system.

  • Founded in 1930
  • Medium of Education: English
  • Annual Tuition Fees: 4,47,230 INR
  • Accreditation: WHO (World Health Organization), NMC (National Medical Commission)
  • Admission Criteria: NEET Qualified
  • Students Trained: 20,000+
  • Country Ranking: 181th
  • World Ranking: 1200th

About Crimea Federal University

The Crimea Federal University is one of the largest universities in Crimea, Russia. It is also the largest university in Southern Russia and is popular for its tremendous medical education. The university is recognized or approved by the WHO (World Health Organization) and NMC (National Medical Commission).

The university was founded in 1918, the V.I Vernadsky Crimean Federal University. It is located in Simferopol city and is the first medical institution of medical education. It offers a 6-year MBBS program including a 1-year internship. It is listed as a top medical institution in Russia. It is the best choice for students who want to study MBBS in Russia.

  • Founded in: 1918 Medium of Education: English
  • Annual Tuition Fees: 2,50,000 RUB
  • Accreditation: NMC and WHO recognized
  • Admission Criteria: NEET Qualification
  • Students Trained: 2,500+
  • Country Ranking: 18th
  • World Ranking: 3748th

About Northern State Medical University

The Northern State Medical University founded in 1932 in Arkhangelsk, Russia. The university has a rich history of providing high-quality medical education to students from all over the world. The university is recognized by the National Medical Commission (NMC), the Medical Council of India (MCI), and the World Health Organization (WHO), making it a great choice for students those who want to do MBBS in Russia or want to do MBBS abroad.

University provides highly skilled doctorate degree holders every year and it also provides a world-class education system as education is provided by highly skilled faculty and staff. Universities provide scholarships every year according to their academic performance. Hence for completing doctorate courses from a well-recognized or highly-ranked university Northern State Medical University is the best choice.

  • Founded in: 1932
  • Medium of Education: English
  • Medium of Education: English
  • Annual Tuition Fees: 4,53,699 INR
  • Accreditation: NMC, MCI, WHO
  • Admission Criteria: NEET Qualified
  • Students Trained: 4K to 6K annually
  • Country Ranked: 178th
  • World Ranking: 5301st

About Tver State Medical University

The university was founded in 1902 with the dental course for the students. Later it starts the medical Tver State Medical University program for students. The Tver State Medical University is recognized and approved by WHO (World Health Organization) and NMC (National Medical Commission). So if someone is looking to study MBBS in Russia then Tver State Medical University is a perfect choice. University is also the best place for those students who want to study MBBS abroad as they are unsatisfied with their country's education system.

University has highly skilled faculty and staff that give high-quality education to the students so that they can’t face any problems in the field of the medical program. University also provides courses at an affordable cost or lesser money which may help those students who are financially weak.
