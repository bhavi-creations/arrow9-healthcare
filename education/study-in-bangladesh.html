MBBS IN
BANGLADESH

Continuing the medical program from abroad medical universities is in trend among students all over the world. Selecting the best and most comfortable location is the priority of most of the candidates. This is where MBBS in Bangladesh comes in; the nearest abroad destination for Indian students to complete their medical education.

Bangladesh is the home of so many reputed Government and Private medical colleges that offer the MBBS program to students. These medical colleges got approval from NMC & WHO and were listed among the best Medical colleges in Bangladesh.

Quality medical educations, affordable MBBS Fees, lots of accommodations, etc. are some of the major factors that attract students to Study MBBS in Abroad at Bangladesh. Medical colleges take care of students and provide them with better facilities.

Bangladesh Facts

Bangladesh is one of the Neighboring countries of India to study the MBBS program. Medical colleges in the country offer the leading program, i.e. Medical Program to students. All the mbbs college in Bangladesh focus on providing the best infrastructure and satisfactory medical education to students.

Glimpses of MBBS in Bangladesh 2022

1

The VISA Process to study the MBBS program in Bangladesh take 10-15 working days. It can be renewed after 6 months.

2

There is no VISA requirement for Indian students to study the MBBS program.Medical colleges offering the MBBS program in Bangladesh is recognized by NMC, WHO, ECFMG, FAIMER, etc.

3

Bangladesh is the right place to complete the MBBS degree in affordable tuition fees with world-class facilities.

4

1 Bangladeshi Taka = 0.88 Indian Rupee (keep an eye on the latest currency rates before applying for admission).

5

Nearest abroad destination gives students to reach at their favorite college in limited time duration.

6

Bangladesh Medical Colleges follow the NMC gazette so it’s a great decision to apply for admission at Bangladeshi medical college.

7

The MBBS course starts in the mid and end of October month every year in Bangladesh. The duration of the medical program is 5.5 year, which is similar to India.

Above are some of the major facts that make Bangladesh unique from other foreign countries. These facts attract Indian students to pursue the MBBS program.

NMC / MCI Approved Medical Colleges in Bangladesh


Bangladesh Medical College

Founded in 1986, Bangladesh Medical College is the oldest private medical institute in Bangladesh. It is approved by Bangladesh Medical and Dental council and a best choice to study MBBS in Bangladesh.


Barind Medical College

Barind Medical College in Bangladesh is one of the best private medical colleges in Bangladesh. It was founded in 2011 and become the prestigious medical institution for MBBS study in Bangladesh.


Manipal College of Medical Sciences, Nepal

The Manipal College of Medical Sciences was founded in 1994 and is affiliated with Kathmandu University. It was the first private institution to establish a medical college in Nepal.


Dhaka National Medical College

The history of Dhaka National Medical College is start from 1925 and since then it became the best medical institution for MBBS study. It has its own Hospital to provide clinical practice to medical students.


BGC Trust Medical College, Bangladesh

BGC Trust Medical College Bangladesh is a famous medical institution in Bangladesh. The journey of BGCTMCB start from 2003 and since then it became the preferred choice of most of the foreign students.


Eastern Medical College

Founded in 2005, the Eastern medical College is a private medical institution to provide the best medical program in Bangladesh. It is a perfect choice to study MBBS in Bangladesh for Indian students.


Khawaja Yunus Ali Medical College

The Khawaja Yunus Ali Medical College was founded in 2005 to deliver the MBBS program in the region. It has all the facilities for delivering the best medical program to become a medical expert.
