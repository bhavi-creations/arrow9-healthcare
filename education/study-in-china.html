MBBS IN CHINA

China, one of the world’s most ancient civilizations, has recorded a history of around 4,000 years. It is the home of ancient higher medical education and has so many oldest medical institutions. Students love to study MBBS in China because of higher medical education and a world-class lifestyle.

China is the first preference of many students around the world not only for quality education but also for “Traditional Medical Education”. The TME is an ancient system of health and wellness that’s been used in China for thousands of years.

Western medicine pores mainly on treating disease, but TCM looks at your entire well-being. We have pointed out some other facts to choose China for your abroad study.

COVID-19 Updates
Because of the global pandemic and effective control of COVID-19 in China, so many students plan to study MBBS in China. Officially, there is no announcement from China’s Ministry of Education or any other government body on when students are able to enter China. Currently, classes in NMC Approved Medical Universities in China are taught online for students who are not available in the country.

China facts

The world’s most populous place, China is officially known as the People’s Republic of China. It is the home of one of the oldest civilizations. It is the 3rd world’s largest country that has five different time zones and borders with 14 different countries. Hong Kong is a city in China that has more skyscrapers than any other city in the world.

Study MBBS in China vs MBBS in India

MBBS In China MBBS In India
MBBS Fees in China is between INR 20/- lakh to INR 40/- lakh. MBBS in India Fees is between INR 50/- lakh to INR 1/- cr.
The student’s teacher ratio at Top Medical Universities in China is 17:1. Teachers –Students ratio is extremely high due to high strength in a class.
World-ranked medical universities are available for MBBS study. No Indian medical college is listed among the top medical colleges in the World.
Regular Practical classes for clinical practice. Few Medical Colleges in India have better facilities for clinical practice.
No donation or capitation is required for MBBS Admission in China. A donation amount is required for MBBS Admission in India at Private Colleges.

NMC / MCI Approved Medical Colleges In China


Hebei Medical University, China

The oldest and AAA graded university situated in Shijiazhuang, Hebei, P. R. China. It is established in 1894and has long records of international students who are coming from other countries for medical courses.


Capital Medical University

The Capital Medical University was formerly known as the Beijing second medical college. It was founded in 1960 and ranked among the top medical institution in China


Dalian Medical University

One of the top universities in China, the Dalian Medical University, provides medical education since 1947. Latest technology fewer hospitals are available for better clinical medical education.


Jilin University, China

Jilin University is a prestigious medical university in China founded in 1946. It is famous for providing the best medical education to its candidates.


Fudan University, China

Fudan University is famous among Indian Medical students who want to study MBBS in China. It was founded in the year 1905 and established in Shanghai. The university has an impressive history of providing world-class education.


Nanjing Medical University, China

Nanjing Medical University China is one of the key universities in the Jiangsu province established in 1934. The university is approved by the Ministry of Education, the National Health and Family Planning Commission, and the Jiangsu Provincial Government.


Anhui Medical University, China

Anhui Medical University is one of the top medical universities in China to study the MBBS program. It was founded in 1926 in Shanghai city. The medical council of India recognizes the Anhui Medical University including the world health organization, and other developed nations.
