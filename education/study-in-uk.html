FAQS

  • Q1. How is studying in the UK different from India?

      • Quality education with best universities such as King's College, Liverpool University, Queen Mary university of London, New Castle University, University of Bristol, University of Manchester.
      • Mixed culture of more than 130 countries with thousands of students all over the World.
      • Degrees are recognised all over the world.
      • Affordable tuition fee as compared to the value of Education.
      • Increased job opportunities in the UK after brexit.

  • Q2. What is the duration of course study in the UK as compared to India?

      UK is known for its short duration of study rather than any other country in the World. It completes a full graduation in just 3 years and post-graduation in just 1 year. While India takes 3-4 years to complete graduation and 2 year to complete post-graduation.

  • Q3. How can the fee payments be made by candidates?

      Fee payment for the engineering exams can be made by cash, card, NEFT, Net Banking, or UPI.

  • Q4. Is the eligibility criteria flexible enough to include candidates whove slightly missed out on the criteria?

      No! Until and unless candidates dont meet the requisite eligibility criteria, they cannot appear for any of the engineering exams.

  • Q5. How can we manage our expenses while studying?

      We can work part time job in the UK. Government allows for 20 hours/week. We can get extra skills by working at different places, as well as working at internships. Wage includes from 9.5 - 17 per hour. Hence students don't need to be stressed about the expenses in the UK.

  • Q6. Does UK education provide only specific courses?

      No, UK education does not provide only specific courses, instead it provides a wide series of courses that are offered to be studied. Even it includes the courses that are associated with the personal hobbies such as dance, music and many others.

  • Q7. Can I Study in the UK without the IELTS Exam?

      Yes, you can Study in the UK without IELTS as most of the universities in the country have decided to ease the pressure on international students. They do not need to take the IELTS Exam. Its consider your class 12th Board results
