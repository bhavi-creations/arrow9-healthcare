Courses Eligibility Criteria

Firstly, Students need to meet the Courses Eligibility Criteria to study the popular courses available in different programs. All the courses have their criteria and individual entrance exams which students need to qualify to get a seat in the course.

Students need to clear the entrance examination based on the program. There are so many top colleges in India that conduct their entrance examination for admission to the college. We have listed below the eligibility criteria for all the courses available for Indian applicants.

Eligibility Criteria for Management Courses

To pursue an MBA, complete at least 3 years of Bachelor Degree in any stream and score 50% of marks (for general category)

Final year To pursue BBA, complete the 12th class education with commerce stream and score above 50% of marks

Final year To pursue BBA, complete the 12th class education with commerce stream and score above 50% of marks

Graduate students are also eligible

Eligibility Criteria for Engineering Courses

To study the BE/ B.Tech. applicants need to pass the 12th class exam with a 50% score with PCM as main subjects

To study ME/ M.Tech. Applicants need to finish the undergraduate program in BE/ B Tech Degree

Final year graduate students are also eligible

Exams - JEE Main, JEE Advanced, BITSAT, VITEEE, COMEDK-UGET, KCET, KEAM, IPU CET, NATA, WBJEE, MHTCET, AP EAMCET, UPSEE, etc.

Eligibility Criteria for Computer Courses

To pursue BCA, students need to pass the 12th class and score 50% of marks with Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics as main subjects

To pursue MCA, applicants need to pass the bachelors degree in the same field with above 50% of the score

Eligibility Criteria for medical Courses

To pursue MBBS or BDS, applicants need to pass the 12th class examination with Physics, Chemistry, and Biology as main subjects

Students need to score at least 50% of the score in the 12th class to pursue the MBBS course

To pursue MD/MS, applicants need to complete an undergraduate degree in the same field with a good score

Exams NEET PG and NEET UG
