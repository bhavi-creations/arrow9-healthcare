Tashkent Medical Academy

Overview

Overview: Tashkent Medical Academy is a non-profit public higher education institution located in Tashkent city, Uzbekistan. It is one of the biggest universities in Uzbekistan. Which is also the capital of Uzbekistan. Established in 2005, which boosts the higher education system and availability of a well-versed working environment. The estimated admission rate ranges between 60-70%. It aims to build leadership qualities amongst its students globally. It aims to project the idea of studying Mbbs Abroad. Many medical admirers from worldwide aim to study Mbbs in Uzbekistan.

The university has majorly extended into the biggest universities of Uzbekistan, which has 177 teachers, including 25 professors, 26 associate professors, 104 assistants, and 1099 students. 

Recently, 6249 students are studying at this institution; 5,441 undergraduates & 808 postgraduate students at Tashkent Medical Academy.

The University faculty is highly trained and educated, it offers upskilled and competent based learning among the students. Students excel in every field of medicine globally. 

The university doesn't restrict its boundaries towards academic aptitude but lays its footprints in social work as well, they are well-versed and have an active participation in goodwill deeds.

Teachers impart skillful teachings to the students, making updated learning sources and also focussing on the epitome of knowledge.

Eligibility Criteria

Tashkent Medical Academy Eligibility Criteria

Tashkent Medical Academy offers well-versed programs in the medical profession. To be eligible according to the programs it avails.

Criteria

Indian students are mandated to qualify for their NEET UG exam.

Academics
1. 50% marks for General Category in 12th standard.
2. 40% marks for Reservation Category.
3. Students must have Physics, Chemistry, and Biology in their 12th standards.
Age

Students must be 17 by 31st December in the year of enrollment of the course.

Admission

Documents Required

10th & 12th mark sheet

Applicants form and visa international form

Health and fitness certificates

NEET Scorecard of the candidate

Original Passport

Passport size photographs

COVID-19 Report (Negative) & HIV Report
